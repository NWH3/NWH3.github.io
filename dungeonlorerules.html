Dark Elf, kin to the Elves, were forced underground long ago due to the results of warring kingdoms. Their white hair and dark skin easily distinguishes them from their Elven brothers and sisters. They rely on their agility and cunning to survive in the dark depths of the realm. Walking a different path than the Elves that walk the surface, the Dark Elves still share their spiritual inclination. Just for a vastly different spiritual goal.

Dwarves are a small, stout, strong, and resourceful race that dwell deep beneath the ground building magnificent fortresses and crafting powerful items. Fighting and war is a common occupation of the burly people as resources are difficult to find and mine.

Elves are a tall, slender, agile, and spiritually inclined race that live within the great forests, plains, and mountains that cover the realm. It is because of the direct actions of the Elves that the trees, plants, and wildlife flourish and thrive. There are many different types of elves that live in various types of environments. The High Elves of the floating castles, the nomadic tribes of the Wild Elves, the agricultural Forest Elves and Sun Elves who built large thriving cities, and the hermit Moon Elves of the sunken valleys and mountains. Many healing powers and spiritual enlightenment is spread from the Elven tribes to the realm.
